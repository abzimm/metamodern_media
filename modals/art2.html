“Space” - Alfred Stoll

Stoll's artwork is defined by metamodernism–he values the remixing of materials and aesthetics, using both digital and traditional mediums in his work. He calls it a “metamodern balance”, where there's neither black nor white, but only the “fluctuations between them”. This fragmented style can be seen in Space's combination of older mosaic patterns mixed with modern stylings.
